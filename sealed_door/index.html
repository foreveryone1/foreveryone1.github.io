the Unsealing of the Door

Before you stands the entrance of a vast keep, half buried in rubble and discarded tools.

The keep, once a sanctuary for peace between seasons, now repurposed into a ceremonial battleground, has been sealed since your initial Faustian deal that wreaked havoc on the Feywild.

With the aid of Pando and the Dryads, and the Wooly Loxodon tribe, you have managed to create a confluence of significantly powerful Winter and Summer magic. The gate sealing the keep has opened itself once again.

In various scripts and styles you can see images and sentences carved into the hallway. Though not all of them are immediately legible, all have a tangible aura of power to them, one of prophecy, or perhaps ill omen.

This realm within a realm, consecrated and given a power strange and terrifying, where countless armistices have been carved out and broken between the seasons now lies before you. Do you dare venture within and remake reality one final time?
